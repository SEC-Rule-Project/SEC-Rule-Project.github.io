What is the SEC?

The SEC(Securities and Exchange Commission) is a federal agency which enforces the law against market manipulation.
When the SEC embarks on its rulemaking duties, it publishes a concept release to the federal register. The public will then submit comments on the proposed rule which are reviewed by the SEC.
Commenters range from big banks like Goldman Sachs to individual investors. Certain rules are finalized while others are not, and some rules are similar to original proposal while others change significantly.
